Case Discussion Board

I built an interactive Case Discussion Board for in-class case discussions. The instructor structures a case into sequential questions, and students respond live on their devices. Responses appear on a shared board projected in the classroom, supporting sentiment word clouds, open-ended text responses, and live polls.
