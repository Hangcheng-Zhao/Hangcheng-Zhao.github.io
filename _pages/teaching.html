Case Discussion Board

To make case discussions more interactive and engaging, I built a Case Discussion Board web app. The instructor structures a case into sequential questions, and students respond live on their devices. Responses appear on a shared board projected in the classroom, supporting sentiment word clouds, open-ended text responses, and live polls. The project is open source on GitHub.
